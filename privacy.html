Echova Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

Echova ("Echova," "we," "us," or "our") provides messaging and AI presence features, including Chat, Contacts, Moments, AI Echo, and account services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal data when you use Echova.

By using Echova, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Echova mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Account and Identity Data

  • Email address and login credentials (or authentication tokens)
  • Account identifiers (user ID)
  • Optional profile information you provide (display name, profile image, status)

2.2 Contacts and Social Graph Data

  • Friend requests and friend relationship status
  • Blocked user list
  • User-to-user permission settings

2.3 Chat Content

  • Message content sent in human chat and AI Echo chat
  • Message metadata (timestamps, sender/receiver IDs, delivery/read state where supported)
  • Safety and moderation signals generated from content (e.g., abuse/risk flags)

2.4 Media Upload Data

  • Media files you choose to upload (such as images, audio, video, and attachments where enabled)
  • File metadata (file type, size, upload status, storage URL)

2.5 Moments and User-Generated Content (UGC)

  • Posts, comments, likes, and related metadata
  • Reports, blocks, and moderation actions

2.6 AI Echo and AI Persona Data

  • Prompts and responses in AI Echo conversations
  • AI persona configuration data (for example, selected AI mode/settings)
  • Memory and context elements required to provide continuity features

2.7 Technical and Device Data

  • Device/app version, language settings, crash logs, diagnostics
  • Session and security logs required for account protection and service reliability

3. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide core features (messaging, contacts, moments, AI Echo, account access)
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Deliver and store chat/media content
  • Generate AI responses and maintain AI continuity features
  • Prevent abuse, harassment, fraud, and unsafe use
  • Support report/block/remove workflows
  • Improve reliability, debugging, and product quality
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Processing (AI Echo and AI Persona)

Echova includes AI-generated responses.

  • AI Echo responses are generated by automated systems.
  • AI Echo is not a real person and does not represent a real person.
  • We may process message inputs and relevant context to generate AI outputs.
  • We may apply safety filters and policy checks to prompts and responses.
  • We do not use private user content for third-party advertising personalization.

If model providers are used, processing may occur through trusted infrastructure providers under contractual controls and applicable law.

4.1 Model Training Policy

  • By default, private chat content is not used to train public third-party foundation models.
  • If Echova introduces any optional training or personalization program that uses user content beyond service delivery, we will provide clear notice and any legally required consent mechanism in advance.
  • Safety and abuse-detection signals may be processed to protect users and platform integrity.

5. Chat Content and Media

  • Chat messages and uploaded media are processed and stored to deliver messaging functionality.
  • Content may be stored until deleted by the user, removed under policy, or expired under retention rules.
  • "Private" conversations are intended to be visible according to product permission boundaries (for example, based on your configured privacy and access controls).

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data.

We may disclose data only:

  • To service providers acting on our behalf (hosting, storage, analytics, security, support)
  • To comply with legal process or law enforcement requests where legally required
  • To enforce Terms of Service, investigate abuse, or protect user/public safety
  • In connection with merger, acquisition, or asset transfer (with appropriate notice where required)

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to:

Provide the Service

  • Maintain account and security integrity
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Meet legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations

Retention periods may vary by data type (e.g., account records, messages, moderation records, logs).
