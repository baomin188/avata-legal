Account

How do I delete my account?

Open Echova, go to Settings → Account, and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, messages, and Echo data. You can also request deletion by emailing us at the address above.

How do I update my email or profile?

Go to Settings → Account in the app to update your profile information.

Subscriptions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Echova subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. To manage, change, or cancel a subscription: open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Echova, then choose an option. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request one.
